Description: This is an openGL animation with cute character and nice scenery. It talks about a warm story that two seagulls are flying over the beautiful ocean and suddenly met each other. This two seagulls are attracted by each other and flying closer, then fall in love.

Custom shape: 1) Heart. 2) Text.

Camera: using look_at() function make the camera closer and closer to main characters,smoothly making the camera view from the whole ocean picture to specific characters focus.Camera view is changing automatically according to time, no input needed.

Hierarchical object: created seagull with three levels.

Texture: sky and ocean.

Speed: real-time speed.

Frame rate: use live_string() function to record the flying height of seagulls.
